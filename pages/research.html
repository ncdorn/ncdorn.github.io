My work focuses on computational hemodynamics in the pulmonary circulation: coupling 3D CFD with 0D/1D LPN models, structured-tree microvascular boundary conditions, and microvascular adaptation models for predictive surgery planning.

Interests

  • Pulmonary circulation modeling and waveform fidelity
  • Structured-tree outflow boundary conditions and impedance tuning
  • Adaptive radius/thickness models under shear and transmural stress
  • Coupling svZeroDSolver with svMultiPhysics (SimVascular)

Methods

  • Patient-specific 3D CFD with RANS/transient solvers
  • Closed-loop LPN circulations with RCR and structured trees
  • Sensitivity analyses, parameter sweeps, uncertainty quantification
